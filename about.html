About Flecto

What is Flecto?

Flecto is a free hub for powerful online tools and verified discount codes — all in one place. Our mission is simple: help people work smarter and spend less, completely free, no signup required.

We believe powerful tools and good deals should be accessible to everyone — whether you are a student in Pakistan, a freelancer in the UK, or a business owner anywhere in the world.
